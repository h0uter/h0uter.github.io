Welcome to my WIP portfolio site!

I hold a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Delft University of Technology and am currently finishing my master’s degree in Cognitive Robotics.

During my Bachelor I worked for Skelex and I am gratefull to have visited many industrial tech events to demonstrate their passive exo-skeleton and speak with other companies on their behalf. I really enjoyed investigating the usecases of potential customers. Often times the exo-skeletons would be used for physcially demanding jobs for which ‘the robot’ was not good enough yet.

My general academic interests relate to the learning, reasoning and planning aspects of artificial agents. These seems to be the core challenges to bring robotics ‘into the wild’.

I also really enjoy looking at nature from an engineering perspective, because it inspires me on what is possible.
